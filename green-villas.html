Project Overview

Green Villas is an exclusive gated villa community in Vadavalli, Coimbatore, offering spacious 3 & 4 BHK homes with private gardens. Surrounded by greenery and designed with modern architecture, this project promises peaceful luxury living for discerning homeowners.

Key Highlights

  • Individual Villas with Private Gardens
  • Vaastu Compliant Layouts
  • Clubhouse with Swimming Pool
  • 24x7 Security & CCTV Monitoring
  • Covered Car Parking
  • Solar Water Heaters & Rainwater Harvesting
  • Power Backup for Common Areas
  • Gym, Yoga Deck & Indoor Games
